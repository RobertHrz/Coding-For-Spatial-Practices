Contrato Natural

A landscape proposal that puts nature first.

Project Overview

Contrato Natural is a landscape proposal that prioritizes the use of natural materials and native plant species. The project was designed to create a sustainable and harmonious relationship between humans and nature.

Key Features

  • Use of natural materials
  • Native plant species
  • Sustainable design
  • Harmonious relationship with nature
